External User Manual 


Home page

On the home page, you can find all latest news, along with twitter feed and links to all MAIA social media channels.

The Timeline shows the closest upcoming events on the MAIA calendar.

About MAIA

In this section you can read more about MAIA project, its objectives and how the work is distributed in work packages.


Partners

All the information regarding MAIA’s partners, who they are and from which countries, you can find on an interactive map on the Partners page.


The second part of the Partners page presents the Advisory board members.

Library

In this section you can find all MAIA publications, related to the project documents and the project deliverables.

You have the option to filter which files you want to view by clicking on the ↕ sign of the drop down menu. 

All of the documents can be downloaded.


News

All recent news about the project can be found on the News section.


Links

On the Links page, you can find all useful links with information relevant to MAIA.


Account Explorer

Under this section you can view the Ecosystem Accounts that have been developed within the ten MAIA countries. Accounts from outside the MAIA consortium will also be available for review.


Events

On the Events section, you can find the calendar with all MAIA organised and involved upcoming events. More information regarding a specific event, you can read by clicking on it.



Media Center

The Media center is a storage space for all project promotional tools, including logo, flyer, presentations, press releases and videos. You can view each of the items by clicking on the folder of your choice. All of the marketing materials can be downloaded.

Contacts

The Contact page presents the business cards of the project coordinator and manager.


Internal Repository - User manual

Log In 

Each user will be provided with individual log in credentials, with which you can access the internal repository part of the website.



After being logged in, you are presented with six folders consisted of the internal information/documentation of MAIA.


Administrative documents:

By clicking on each one of the thumbnails you will be redirected to the respective folder:

By clicking on the + sign at the left corner you will be presented with a subfolder, consisted of all the available documentation. All of the files can be downloaded. (this is applicable to all of the six folders in the MAIA Repository):

To create a new new folder or directly upload files to the already existing ones, choose the option “Create folder”/ “Upload files”. (this is applicable to all of the six folders in the MAIA Repository):

You have the option of creating subfolders or uploading files to the already existing ones. (this is applicable to all of the six folders in the MAIA Repository):

If you wish to rename an existing folder and\or delete one, you can do so by clicking with the right button of your mouse (cursor) over the respective folder. (this is applicable to all of the six folders in the MAIA Repository):


NB: All actions on files and folders, such as the editing or deleting, can be performed only by the user who created them.
